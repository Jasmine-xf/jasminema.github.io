Textile Museum of Canada

As a museum dedicated exclusively to the textile arts, this redesign focuses on navigation, engagement and interaction for younger, diverse audiences through a modern, virtual experience.

Category
Navigation Design
Type
Group Project
Duration
1 Month

Problem Statement

How might we redesign the Textile Museum of Canada's website for easy navigation and enhance its appeal to a younger, diverse audience through a virtual museum experience?

Goal

Renovate the structure and navigation design into a modern site emphasizing visitors’ engagement and interaction, aligning with stakeholder’s core focus.

Clear information structure for intuitive access

Virtual experience to explore exhibitions

Appeal to younger, diverse audiences
